Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FirstMod-AI

What features are available for free users? +

Free users have access to Face Swap functionality, allowing you to swap faces between images. This is a great way to explore our AI technology and create fun, creative content. However, advanced features like FOMD (First Order Motion Model) and MakeItTalk require a subscription.

What features are included with a paid subscription? +

Subscribers get access to all premium features including:

  • FOMD (First Order Motion Model) - Transfer motion and expressions from videos to your images
  • MakeItTalk - Synchronize audio with video to make faces talk naturally
  • Face Swap - All the free features plus priority processing
  • Faster processing times and priority support
  • Unlimited animations and downloads

How do I create an account on FirstMod-AI? +

Creating an account is quick and easy:

  1. Click the "Sign Up" button in the top navigation or on the homepage
  2. Fill in your details: Full Name, Email Address, and Password
  3. Verify your email address (check your inbox for a verification link)
  4. Once verified, you can log in and start using our free features immediately
  5. To access premium features, upgrade to a subscription from your dashboard

How long does it take to process animations? +

Processing times vary depending on the feature and complexity:

  • Face Swap: Typically 30 seconds to 2 minutes
  • FOMD (First Order Motion Model): Usually 1-3 minutes depending on video length
  • MakeItTalk: Generally 2-4 minutes for audio synchronization

Subscribers receive priority processing, which can reduce wait times significantly. You'll see a progress indicator during processing, and you'll be notified when your animation is ready!
